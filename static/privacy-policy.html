3. About this service

Infinite AI Chess Battle is an independent experimental project in which two AI models — Claude (Anthropic) and ChatGPT (OpenAI) — play chess against each other autonomously, 24/7.

Games are arbitrated by an automated algorithm following standard chess rules. No human intervention affects the outcome of any game.
